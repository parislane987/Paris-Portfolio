About Me

Hi! My name is Paris Lane. I'm 20 years old, live in Alamogordo, NM, and am currently attending NMSU-A for an Associate's degree in graphic design.I currently work as a Search Quality Rater for the company RWS Global, where I basically judge the quality of any given website and rate it using a rating system. Having a job like this, and having taken many classes based around design programs, means I spend a lot of time around computers and online. My interests include creating art both digitally and traditionally, photo editing, music, video games, and anything graphic design. I have always loved creating cohesive designs and aesthetics for stuff, which is what made coding this portfolio website fun for me!

Currently, I aim to start my own graphic design business designing logos, branding, and other things for anyone who needs it. It is my dream to be able to express my creativity and share it with the world. I would love to see my designs being used by a big company one day!
