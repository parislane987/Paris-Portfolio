McGinn's Pistachioland

These are two logos I created with Adobe Illustrator in my Illstrator class here at NMSU. We were supposed to find a business that could use a branding re-up, and I picked our nice local pistachio farm in Alamogordo. For the first logo, I went for a more traditional looking art style that conveyed the farm's two major products: pistachios, and wine from the Arena Blanca Winery. The second logo is my personal favorite -- for this one I went for a more modern, natural look with a sunset and pistachio branches.
